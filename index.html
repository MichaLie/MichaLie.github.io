FAIR data note

These catalogs are generated from versioned canonical data and provide synchronized JSON, JSON Schema, and JSON-LD metadata with stable identifiers, provenance, and evidence dates. FAIR describes the catalogs—not every resource they index. Each entry remains a discovery aid; verify current licences, availability, and terms at the cited source.
